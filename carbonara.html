Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

2 servings

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 100g pancetta
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper

Method

  • Boil salted water in a large saucepan
  • Dice pancetta into small cubes
  • Mix the grated parmesan with the egg yolks and black pepper
  • Add olive oil to a frying pan and cook pancetta on a low heat for 8 minutes
  • When spaghetti is al dente, strain but save a mug full of pasta water
  • Take the pancetta off the heat then add the spaghetti with a little bit of pasta water
  • Add the egg and cheese mixture while constantly stiring, adding more pasta water as needed
  • Continue stirring and adding pasta water until the sauce reaches the desired consitency
  • Serve with more grated parmesan and enjoy!
